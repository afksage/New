About Me

I am a certified consulting dietitian with over 8 years of experience helping individuals and families achieve optimal health through personalized nutrition strategies. Based in Ghana, I specialize in managing chronic diseases, promoting wellness, and creating sustainable dietary habits.

My approach combines evidence-based nutrition science with an understanding of local food culture to create practical, achievable nutrition plans.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor of Science in Nutrition & Dietetics
  • Registered Dietitian Nutritionist (RDN)
  • Certified Diabetes Educator (CDE)
  • Sports Nutrition Specialist Certification
