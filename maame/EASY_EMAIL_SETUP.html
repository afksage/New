‚úÖ No backend needed! This method works on any hosting including GitHub Pages.

Step 1: Get Your Email

You'll need an email address where you want to receive contact form submissions.

Examples:

  • Gmail: yourname@gmail.com
  • Business email: name@yourcompany.com
  • Yahoo: name@yahoo.com

Step 2: Update Your Contact Form

Replace the form action in your HTML:

Find this line in index.html:

<form class="contact-form" id="contactForm">

Replace with:

<form action="https://formsubmit.co/your-email@gmail.com" method="POST">

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Replace your-email@gmail.com with YOUR actual email!

Step 3: Update Form Inputs

Make sure your form inputs have name attributes:

<input type="text" name="name" placeholder="Your Name" required>
<input type="email" name="email" placeholder="Your Email" required>
<input type="tel" name="phone" placeholder="Your Phone">
<textarea name="message" placeholder="Your Message" required></textarea>
<button type="submit">Send Message</button>

Step 4: Test It!

  1. Deploy website to Netlify, GitHub, or any host
  2. Go to your website
  3. Fill in contact form
  4. Click Send Message
  5. You'll see a confirmation page
  6. Check your email in 1-2 minutes
  7. May appear in spam - add to contacts

Step 5: Verify Your Email (Optional)

After first submission, FormSubmit will ask you to verify your email.

Follow the link in the verification email to start receiving submissions automatically.

‚úÖ That's It!

All form submissions now come to your email.

No server setup needed.
